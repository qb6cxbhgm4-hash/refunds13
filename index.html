Does RefundOS handle both refunds and inventory?+
Yes. RefundOS has two AI engines: one for refund decisions (approve, deny, or escalate) and one for inventory (predict stockouts, recommend reorder quantities, and auto-execute orders). Both are configurable from suggest-only to full autopilot.
How does the inventory prediction work?+
RefundOS tracks 7-day, 14-day, and 30-day sales velocity per SKU. It factors in supplier lead time, safety stock thresholds, and seasonal patterns to predict when you'll run out — and recommends exactly how much to reorder.
What Shopify data does RefundOS use?+
Orders, customers, products, and inventory levels — read access for analysis, write access for refund processing and inventory updates. We never touch payment details. Revoke access anytime from Shopify admin.
What if the AI makes a wrong refund or reorder decision?+
Every decision is logged with full reasoning. Refunds are reversible within 24 hours. Reorders can be cancelled before supplier confirmation. Fraud-likely and high-value items always escalate to manual review.
Can I control what gets automated?+
Completely. Set automation levels independently for refunds and inventory. Use dollar thresholds, customer tiers, product categories, and risk scores to control what gets auto-processed and what requires your approval.
How fast can I get started?+
Under 2 minutes. Connect your Shopify store via OAuth, set your initial preferences, and RefundOS starts analyzing immediately. No developer, no code, no app store. Cancel anytime with one click.
